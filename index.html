What is Clash Royale?

Clash Royale is a mobile game created by the company supercell

What is so special about it?

It is a game played internationally that is easily accessible to anyone with a mobile or tablet device

How can I play it?

If you have access to a device you can download it from its appstore and start playing for free!

The game is a strategic head to head duel where you create a deck of cards to defeat an opponent who also has fine tuned their deck to defeat you!

The goal is to destroy your opponents crown towers with troops and creatures with unique abilites that all interact in special ways

The more you level up the more cards you can unlock and the more upgrades those cards can receive
